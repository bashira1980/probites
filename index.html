Meet Our Founder

Our Story

ProBite Healthy Snacks was created by Marie-Joe, a sweet-tooth founder who wanted treats that tasted amazing but still felt good to eat.

After struggling to find snacks that were both delicious and nutritious, she decided to create her own. ProBite is made with clean ingredients, no added sugar, and recipes designed to satisfy cravings without compromise.

From protein balls to date bites and cookie dough bites, every ProBite snack is crafted to be guilt-free, nourishing, and satisfying.
